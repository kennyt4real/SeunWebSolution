Oliver Wyman In the Netherlands

 
Walk through our doors and you will find a talented group of passionate entrepreneurs, who also share a good sense of humor. 

Oliver Wyman has been active in the Netherlands from the mid-1990s, building its offering to Dutch financial services, the public domain and retail clients. We currently offer consultancy services across all industries, covering a wide range of capabilities. Oliver Wyman Amsterdam distinguishes itself through specialized industry and functional expertise, rigorous and proven methodologies, a collaborative working style, and agenda-setting research. This work is characterized by exceptional impacts for our clients. By consistently adding value, we have earned a reputation for high-quality work and attractive career opportunities.

Our office in the Netherlands was established in 2013 to serve clients from a local base, with global knowledge and local talent. Since then, we have moved to bigger and bigger premises to accommodate a rapidly expanding workforce, now numbering more than 50 talented individuals representing many different nationalities. In this international space, with its exciting start-up vibe, we’re all contributing in our own way to the company’s development and growth. We pay special attention to personal connections and making everyone feel included.
